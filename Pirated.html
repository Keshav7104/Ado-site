Rules and Regulations

This is the most important part of this whole set of rules! Read this carefully! The object of the Hunt is to have fun. The one thing nobody wants to happen is for a team to be in an accident. Any incident during the Hunt resulting in injury or worse may mean the end of the Hunt. Recognize that success of the Hunt is designed to be based upon clue complexity and your ability to solve them.Win through being prepared, not speeding. The following rules are in the interest of safety. Random spot checks will be done at certain sites by Hunt coordinators. If there is any sign of reckless behaviour as judged by a Hunt coordinator, that team will be disqualified and may not be allowed in future Hunts. All decisions in this matter will be final!

  1. All teams must have at least 2 members and no more than 4 members. you may register in advance by visiting our web page at weselions.weebly.com and completing the on line registration form.
  2. Teams must stay together during the Treasure Hunt and are encouraged to work together to solve the clues and retrieve the coins from each location.
  3. Each team will be given a â€œClue" at the beginning of the treasure hunt. All of the treasure hunt clues will be hidden at different locations throughout our campus. It will be up to the teams to decipher the clues one by one and safely acquire all the clues from each of the locations. Each clue once solved will lead you to a location where you will be able to collect next clue. Whether or not all clues are solved teams must return to Exploration Place by 5:00pm to qualify for prizes.
  4. Each team should have a person who knows the our campus routes
  5. Disqualification will result from any of the following
    1. Reckless Behaviour
    2. Damaging any property
    3. Interfering with other teams or their members in any way
    4. Copying from other teams.
    5. Stealing from other teams .
